Food

Empanada

Argentina - This crispy, half-moon shaped pastry has a hot, savory filling of cheese, meat or vegetables.

Arepa

Venezuela - This flatbread sanwich made from ground maize dough is filled with cheese, meat or eggs.

Ceviche

Peru - Fresh, raw seafood is cured in citrus juice, spiked with some heat from chili peppers, and garnished with onion and cilantro.

Dessert

Alfajor

Argentina and Uruguay - Creamy filling of queso crema and dulce de leche on top of a cookie.

Torta de mil hojas

Chile - Also known as 'the Thousand Layers' or 'Thousand Sheets', this cake might not literally have one thousand layers, but it's still a dessert of gargantuan porportions.

Quesillo

Venezuela - Some call it flan, some call it crème caramel, but in Venezuela, our jiggly egg and condensed milk friend is called quesillo.

Drinks

Colombian coffee

Colombia - The most delicious coffee with coffee beans from Colombia.

Yerba Mate

Argentina and Uruguay - This is typical South American green tea.

Pisco Sour

Peru and Chile - The most famous South American cocktail.
